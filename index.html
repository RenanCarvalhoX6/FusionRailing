A Style for Every Staircase

Strong, stylish, and built to last — without the drama. At Lux Railings, we design and install custom railings that actually make sense for your space. From aluminum and glass to stainless steel, we build pieces that look great, feel solid, and get compliments. Whether it’s a new build or just time for an upgrade, we’ve got you (and your stairs) covered.

Aluminum Railings

Lightweight, strong, and rust-resistant — our aluminum railings are a go-to for clean lines and low-maintenance durability. Simple, stylish, and built to last.

Stainless Steel Railings

Strong, polished, and made to impress — our stainless steel railings combine durability with a modern edge. Ideal for both indoor and outdoor elegance.

Glass Railings

Sleek, modern, and secure — our glass railings open up your space while keeping safety a top priority. Perfect for letting in the light and showing off the view.

Steel Railings

Tough as nails with timeless style — our steel railings are perfect for strength, security, and that classic welded look. Great for custom designs and heavy-duty applications.
